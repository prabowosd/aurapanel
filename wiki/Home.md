## Design Principles

- `Control plane != serving plane`
- `Operational honesty over cosmetic completeness`
- `Zero-trust by default`
- `Deterministic automation`
- `Performance-sensitive paths stay simple`

## Scope

This wiki focuses on real production operations, including:

- install and lifecycle management
- runtime integrations and service boundaries
- operational guardrails and incident workflows
- migration planning and risk control
